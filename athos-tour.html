Mount Athos Pilgrimage Tour

Duration: 7 days / 6 nights

Price: €850 per person

Start Location: Thessaloniki

What's Included:

  • Transportation from Thessaloniki to Uranopoli (minivan)
  • Boat transfer to Mount Athos
  • Overnights in monasteries
  • Meals provided by monasteries
  • Assistance with entry permits

Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Arrival in Thessaloniki
  • Day 2: Transfer to Uranopoli by minivan, then boat to Mount Athos
  • Day 3: Visit Great Lavra Monastery
  • Day 4: Overnight at Vatopedi Monastery
  • Day 5: Hike and visit Dionysiou Monastery
  • Day 6: Return to Thessaloniki
  • Day 7: Departure
